  [1X3.1 [33X[0;0YCells[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YA  triangle-group cells is defined by the quotient group of a triangle group
  [23X\Delta[123X  by  a  subgroup  of translations [23X\Gamma[123X, i.e., a torsion-free normal
  subgroup of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X. Geometrically, this includes
  a  choice  of transversal [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma)[123X characterizing the cell and a

  (proper)  point group of the cell and [23XG_w^+[123X is the stabilizer of a maximally

  [33X[0;0YThe  cell  is  implemented  as  an  object  of  category  [10XTGCellObj[110X with the
  following components:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XGetProperTriangleGroup[102X ([14X3.2-1[114X): the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XCellRelators[102X   ([14X3.2-2[114X):   the  relators  defining  [23X\Delta^+/\Gamma[123X  as
        elements of [23X\Delta^+[123X[133X

        [23XG^+\cong\Delta^+/\Gamma[123X[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XTGCellMSWPs[102X  ([14X3.2-6[114X): the maximally symmetric Wyckoff positions in the

  [33X[0;0Yand is printed in the form[133X

  [1X3.6 [33X[0;0YMaximally Symmetric Wyckoff Positions[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe maximally symmetric Wyckoff positions in a triangle-group cell are given
  by  the orbital stabilizer theorem as the coset representatives of the right
  cosets of the stabilizer of a maximally symmetric Wyckoff position of type [23Xw[123X
  in  the (proper) point group [23XG^+[123X, i.e., as elements of the right transversal
  [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X.  Here  the  types  of maximally symmetric Wyckoff positions,
  [23Xw=x,y,z[123X,   are  distinguished  by  the  order  of  their  stabilizer  group:
  [23X|G_x^+|=r[123X, [23X|G_y^+|=q[123X, and [23X|G_z^+|=p[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThis  is  implemented  as  an  object  of  category  [10XTGCellMSWPsObj[110X with the
  following components:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XAsQuotient[102X  ([14X3.6-1[114X):  the  list  of  the  three  quotients  [23XG^+/G_w^+[123X,
        [23Xw=x,y,z[123X, as [10XRightTransversal[110X objects[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XGetRightTransversal[102X  ([14X3.6-2[114X): the list of the three right transversals
        [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X,  [23Xw=x,y,z[123X,  with  representatives  as  elements  of the
        proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[2XGetKernel[102X  ([14X3.6-3[114X):  the  list of the three kernels [23XG_w^+[123X, [23Xw=x,y,z[123X, as
        ordered lists of elements of the proper point group [23XG^+[123X[133X
  
  [33X[0;0Yand is printed in the form[133X
  
  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X

  [4X[32X[104X
  
  [33X[0;0Ywhere   [10XTGGx[110X,   [10XTGGy[110X,   [10XTGGz[110X  are  the  right  transversals  [23XT_{G^+}(G_x^+)[123X,
  [23XT_{G^+}(G_y^+)[123X, [23XT_{G^+}(G_z^+)[123X, respectively.[133X

  [33X[1;0Y[29X[2XAsQuotient[102X( [3Xobj[103X ) [32X operation[133X
