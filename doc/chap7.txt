  
  [1X7 [33X[0;0YFile Formats[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe following file formats are introduced by the HyperCells package:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XHyperCell  cell  graph[113X  ([10X*.hcc[110X)  for  storing  the  cell graphs, i.e.,
        [10XTGCellGraph[110X objects (see [14X3.7[114X)[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XHyperCell  model  graph[113X  ([10X*.hcm[110X)  for  storing the model graphs, i.e.,
        [10XTGCellModelGraph[110X objects (see [14X4.1[114X)[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XHyperCell  supercell  graph[113X  ([10X*.hcs[110X)  for  storing the supercell model
        graphs, i.e., [10XTGSuperCellModelGraph[110X objects (see [14X5.1[114X)[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThese file formats are described in detail below.[133X
  
  
  [1X7.1 [33X[0;0YHyperCell cell graph (*.hcc)[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  basic file structure of a HyperCell cell graph file is as follows (with
  placeholders marked by [10X<...>[110X):[133X
  
  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X
    [4X[28XHyperCells HCC version 1.0[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <r>, <q>, <p> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <relators> , <center> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> <generators>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<TDGAMMA>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <TGGx>, <TGGy>, <TGGz> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X<vertices>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edges>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edge translations>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<faces>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<boundary>[128X[104X
  [4X[32X[104X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe first line specifies the file format and its version.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe second line gives the signature [23X(r,q,p)[123X of the underlying triangle group
  [23X\Delta(r,q,p)[123X  and  the  third  line  specifies  the triangle-group quotient
  [23X\Delta^+(r,q,p)/\Gamma[123X  by the list of relators [10X<relators>[110X given as elements
  of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X with generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X and the center
  of  the cell given as the integer [10X<center>[110X with [10X1[110X, [10X2[110X, [10X3[110X corresponding to the
  center  lying  at  the  vertex  [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X of the fundamental Schwarz triangle,
  respectively.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe fourth line specifies the generators of the translation group [23X\Gamma[123X. To
  the left of [10X->[110X are the generators of the finitely presented group and to the
  right  the  list  [10X<generators>[110X  of their images in the proper triangle group
  [23X\Delta^+[123X in terms of the generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  fifth  line  gives  the  transversal  [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma)[123X  as  a  list
  [10X<TDGAMMA>[110X  of  elements  of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X and the sixth
  line  specifies the transversals [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X as lists [10X<TGGw>[110X with [23Xw=x,y,z[123X
  ([10Xw=x,y,z[110X)    of    preimages    of    elements   of   the   quotient   group
  [23XG^+\cong\Delta^+/\Gamma[123X  under  the  quotient  homomorphism [23X\Delta^+\to G^+[123X,
  i.e., in terms of the generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YLines  seven to ten specify the vertices, edges, edge translations and faces
  of the cell:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe vertices are given as a list [10X<vertices>[110X with entries of the form [10X[
        w, gi ][110X, where [10Xw[110X is [10X1[110X, [10X2[110X, [10X3[110X corresponding to the vertex [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X of the
        fundamental  Schwarz triangle and [10Xgi[110X is the position of the element in
        the transversal [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edges  are given as a list [10X<edges>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ v1,
        v2,  s  ][110X,  where  [10Xv1[110X  and  [10Xv2[110X  are  the  positions of the vertices in
        [10X<vertices>[110X  connected by the edge, [10Xs[110X is the position of the element in
        [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma)[123X labeling the Schwarz triangle this edge is a part
        of.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edge  translations, i.e., the translations relating the unit cell
        containing  the  first  vertex  ([10Xv1[110X)  to  the unit cell containing the
        second ([10Xv2[110X) are given as a list [10X<edge translations>[110X of elements of the
        finitely  presented  group,  i.e., in terms of the generators given to
        the  left of [10X->[110X in the third line. If [10Xv1[110X corresponds to the vertex [23X(w,
        [g_{u_w}]_w)[123X  and  [10Xv2[110X  to  the vertex [23X(w', [g_{\tilde{u}_{w'}}]_{w'})[123X,
        then [10Xgam[110X corresponds to the translation [23X\gamma[123X such that[133X
  
  
  [24X      [33X[0;6Yg_{\tilde{u}_{w'}} = g_{u_w}\gamma.[133X
  
  [124X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe faces are given as a list [10X<faces>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ edge,
        orientation  ][110X,  where [10Xedge[110X is the position of the edge in [10X<edges>[110X and
        [10Xorientation[110X  is  [10X1[110X or [10X-1[110X depending on whether the edge is traversed in
        the same or opposite direction as the edge given in [10X<edges>[110X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YFinally,  the  eleventh  line  specifies the boundary identifications of the
  cell as a list [10X<boundary>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ d1, d2, e, b, m, gam ][110X,
  where  [10Xd1[110X,  [10Xd2[110X  are elements of [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma)\subset\Delta^+[123X labeling
  the Schwarz triangles this edge is a part of, [10Xe[110X the position in [10X<edges>[110X, [10Xb[110X a
  running integer index of the boundary (identical for identified boundaries),
  [10Xm[110X  either  [10X0[110X or [10X1[110X indicating the orientation of the triangle associated with
  this instance of the boundary (0 for gray and 1 for white Schwarz triangle),
  and [10Xgam[110X the translation relating the given to the identified boundary, as an
  element  of  the  finitely presented group, i.e., in terms of the generators
  given to the left of [10X->[110X in the third line.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe full file structure of a HyperCell cell graph file thus is as follows:[133X
  
  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X
    [4X[28XHyperCells HCC version 1.0[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <r>, <q>, <p> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ <rel1>, <rel2>, ... ], <center> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> [ <generator1>, <generator2>, ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <d1>, <d2>, ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ <gx1>, <gx2>, ... ], [ <gy1>, <gy2>, ... ], [ <gz1>, <gz2>, ... ] ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ w, gi ], ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ v1, v2, s ], ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ gam1, gam2, ... ], ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ edge, orientation ], ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ d1, d2, e, b, m, gam ], ... ][128X[104X
  [4X[32X[104X

  [1X7.2 [33X[0;0YHyperCell model graph (*.hcm)[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe basic file structure of a HyperCell model graph file is as follows (with
  placeholders marked by [10X<...>[110X):[133X
  
  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X
    [4X[28XHyperCells HCM version 1.0[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <r>, <q>, <p> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <relators> , <center> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> <generators>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<TDGAMMA>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <TGGx>, <TGGy>, <TGGz> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X<model type>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<vertices>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edges>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edge translations>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<faces>[128X[104X
  [4X[32X[104X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe first line specifies the file format and its version.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe second line gives the signature [23X(r,q,p)[123X of the underlying triangle group
  [23X\Delta(r,q,p)[123X  and  the  third  line  specifies  the triangle-group quotient
  [23X\Delta^+(r,q,p)/\Gamma[123X  by the list of relators [10X<relators>[110X given as elements
  of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X with generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X and the center
  of  the cell given as the integer [10X<center>[110X with [10X1[110X, [10X2[110X, [10X3[110X corresponding to the
  center  lying  at  the  vertex  [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X of the fundamental Schwarz triangle,
  respectively.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe fourth line specifies the generators of the translation group [23X\Gamma[123X. To
  the left of [10X->[110X are the generators of the finitely presented group and to the
  right  the  list  [10X<generators>[110X  of their images in the proper triangle group
  [23X\Delta^+[123X in terms of the generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  fifth  line  gives  the  transversal  [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma)[123X  as  a  list
  [10X<TDGAMMA>[110X  of  elements  of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X and the sixth

  ([10Xw=x,y,z[110X)    of    preimages    of    elements   of   the   quotient   group
  [23XG^+\cong\Delta^+/\Gamma[123X  under  the  quotient  homomorphism [23X\Delta^+\to G^+[123X,
  i.e., in terms of the generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  seventh  line  specifies  the model type. The following model types are
  currently implemented:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XDerived  graph[113X:  general  model  graph with vertices, edges, and faces
        derived  from  the  cell  graph. The model type is given as [10X[ "VEF", [
        vfs,  efs,  ffs  ]  ][110X,  where  [10Xvfs[110X, [10Xefs[110X, and [10Xffs[110X are lists of integers

        "VEF", [ [ 2 ], [ 1 ], [ 3 ] ] ] ][110X for the [23X\{p,q\}[123X tessellation and as
        [10X[  "TESS",  [  p,  q  ],  [ "VEF", [ [ 3 ], [ 1 ], [ 2 ] ] ] ][110X for the

        details.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XKagome  graph[113X:  kagome  model  graph  derived  from  a cell graph of a
        triangle group. The model type is given as [10X[ "KAGOME", p, [ "VEF", [ [
        1 ], [ 2 ], [ 2, 3 ] ] ] ][110X. See [2XKagomeModelGraph[102X ([14X4.2-4[114X) for details.[133X

  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe vertices are given as a list [10X<vertices>[110X with entries of the form [10X[

        fundamental  Schwarz triangle and [10Xgi[110X is the position of the element in
        the transversal [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edges  are given as a list [10X<edges>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ v1,
        v2,  tag  ][110X,  where  [10Xv1[110X  and  [10Xv2[110X  are the positions of the vertices in
        [10X<vertices>[110X  connected  by  the edge, and [10Xtag[110X is a unique identifier of
        the  edge.  More  details  on  the  tags  are given in the methods for
        constructing different models, see [14X4.2[114X.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edge  translations, i.e., the translations relating the unit cell
        containing  the  first  vertex  ([10Xv1[110X)  to  the unit cell containing the
        second ([10Xv2[110X) are given as a list [10X<edge translations>[110X of elements of the
        finitely  presented  group,  i.e., in terms of the generators given to
        the  left of [10X->[110X in the third line. If [10Xv1[110X corresponds to the vertex [23X(w,
        [g_{u_w}]_w)[123X  and  [10Xv2[110X  to  the vertex [23X(w', [g_{\tilde{u}_{w'}}]_{w'})[123X,

  [24X      [33X[0;6Yg_{\tilde{u}_{w'}} = g_{u_w}\gamma.[133X
  
  [124X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe faces are given as a list [10X<faces>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ edge,
        orientation  ][110X,  where [10Xedge[110X is the position of the edge in [10X<edges>[110X and
        [10Xorientation[110X  is  [10X1[110X or [10X-1[110X depending on whether the edge is traversed in
        the same or opposite direction as the edge given in [10X<edges>[110X.[133X

    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> [ <generator1>, <generator2>, ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <d1>, <d2>, ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ <gx1>, <gx2>, ... ], [ <gy1>, <gy2>, ... ], [ <gz1>, <gz2>, ... ] ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X<model type>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ w, gi ], ... ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ [ v1, v2, tag ], ... ][128X[104X

  [1X7.3 [33X[0;0YHyperCell supercell graph (*.hcs)[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  basic  file  structure  of a HyperCell supercell model graph file is as
  follows (with placeholders marked by [10X<...>[110X):[133X
  
  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X
    [4X[28XHyperCells HCS version 1.0[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <r>, <q>, <p> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <primitive cell relators> , <supercell relators>, <center> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> <pc generators>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<TDGAMMApc>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <TGpcGx>, <TGpcGy>, <TGpcGz> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> <sc generators>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> <sc generators in terms of pc generators>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<TGAMMApcGAMMAsc>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<TDGAMMAsc>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X[ <TGscGx>, <TGscGy>, <TGscGz> ][128X[104X
    [4X[28X<model type>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<vertices>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edges>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<edge translations>[128X[104X
    [4X[28X<faces>[128X[104X
  [4X[32X[104X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe first line specifies the file format and its version.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe second line gives the signature [23X(r,q,p)[123X of the underlying triangle group
  [23X\Delta(r,q,p)[123X  and  the  third  line  specifies  the triangle-group quotient
  [23X\Delta^+(r,q,p)/\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}[123X and [23X\Delta^+(r,q,p)/\Gamma_\mathrm{sc}[123X by
  the  list  of  relators  [10X<primitive cell relators>[110X and [10X<supercell relators>[110X,
  respectively,  given  as elements of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X with
  generators  [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X and the center of the cell given as the integer [10X<center>[110X
  with  [10X1[110X, [10X2[110X, [10X3[110X corresponding to the center lying at the vertex [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X of the
  fundamental Schwarz triangle, respectively.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe   fourth   line  specifies  the  generators  of  the  translation  group
  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}[123X  of  the  primitive  cell.  To  the  left  of  [10X->[110X are the
  generators  of  the  finitely  presented  group  and  to  the right the list
  [10X<generators>[110X  of their images in the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X in terms
  of the generators [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  fifth  line gives the transversal [23XT_{\Delta^+}(\Gamma_\mathrm{pc})[123X as a
  list  [10X<TDGAMMApc>[110X  of elements of the proper triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X and the
  sixth  line  specifies  the transversals [23XT_{G^+_\mathrm{pc}}(G_w^+)[123X as lists
  [10X<TGpcGw>[110X  with  [23Xw=x,y,z[123X  ([10Xw=x,y,z[110X)  of preimages of elements of the quotient
  group  [23XG^+_\mathrm{pc}\cong\Delta^+/\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}[123X  under  the  quotient
  homomorphism  [23X\Delta^+\to  G^+_\mathrm{pc}[123X, i.e., in terms of the generators
  [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YLines seven, ten, and eleven give the same information as lines three, four,
  and   five,   respectively,   but   for   the   supercell  ([23XG^+_\mathrm{sc}[123X,
  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{sc}[123X) instead of the primitive cell. The eighth line specifies
  the   homomorphism  embedding  the  normal  subgroup  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{sc}[123X  in
  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}[123X  with  the  generators  of  the  finitely presented group
  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{sc}[123X  to  the  left  of  [10X->[110X  and  their images in the finitely
  presented  group  [23X\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}[123X  to the right. The ninth line gives the
  corresponding  transversal  [23XT_{\Gamma_\mathrm{pc}} (\Gamma_\mathrm{sc})[123X as a
  list [10X<TGAMMApcGAMMAsc>[110X of elements of the triangle group [23X\Delta^+[123X.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YThe  twelfth  line  specifies  the model type. The following model types are
  currently implemented:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XDerived  graph[113X:  general  model  graph with vertices, edges, and faces
        derived  from  the  cell  graph. The model type is given as [10X[ "VEF", [
        vfs,  efs,  ffs  ]  ][110X,  where  [10Xvfs[110X, [10Xefs[110X, and [10Xffs[110X are lists of integers
        between  1  and  3  indicating  the vertex types, edge types, and face
        types, respectively. See [2XTGCellModelGraph[102X ([14X4.2-1[114X) for details.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XTessellation graph[113X: tessellation model graph derived from a cell graph
        of  a triangle group. The model type is given as [10X[ "TESS", [ p, q ], [
        "VEF", [ [ 2 ], [ 1 ], [ 3 ] ] ] ][110X for the [23X\{p,q\}[123X tessellation and as
        [10X[  "TESS",  [  p,  q  ],  [ "VEF", [ [ 3 ], [ 1 ], [ 2 ] ] ] ][110X for the
        (dual)  [23X\{q,p\}[123X  tessellation.  See [2XTessellationModelGraph[102X ([14X4.2-2[114X) for
        details.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[13XKagome  graph[113X:  kagome  model  graph  derived  from  a cell graph of a
        triangle group. The model type is given as [10X[ "KAGOME", p, [ "VEF", [ [
        1 ], [ 2 ], [ 2, 3 ] ] ] ][110X. See [2XKagomeModelGraph[102X ([14X4.2-4[114X) for details.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YLines thirteen to sixteen specify the vertices, edges, edge translations and
  faces of the cell:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe vertices are given as a list [10X<vertices>[110X with entries of the form [10X[
        w, gi ][110X, where [10Xw[110X is [10X1[110X, [10X2[110X, [10X3[110X corresponding to the vertex [23Xx[123X, [23Xy[123X, [23Xz[123X of the
        fundamental  Schwarz triangle and [10Xgi[110X is the position of the element in
        the transversal [23XT_{G^+}(G_w^+)[123X.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edges  are given as a list [10X<edges>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ v1,
        v2,  tag  ][110X,  where  [10Xv1[110X  and  [10Xv2[110X  are the positions of the vertices in
        [10X<vertices>[110X  connected  by  the edge, and [10Xtag[110X is a unique identifier of
        the  edge. The tag [10Xtag[110X is of the form [10X[ v1pc, v2pc, tagpc ][110X with [10Xv1pc[110X,
        [10Xv2pc[110X  the  positions  of the vertices in the primitive cell, and [10Xtagpc[110X
        the  tag  of  the  edge  in  the  primitive  cell (see [14X4.2[114X). Thus, [10Xtag[110X
        specifies (up to the translation) the edge in the primitive cell.[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe  edge  translations, i.e., the translations relating the unit cell
        containing  the  first  vertex  ([10Xv1[110X)  to  the unit cell containing the
        second ([10Xv2[110X) are given as a list [10X<edge translations>[110X of elements of the
        finitely  presented  group,  i.e., in terms of the generators given to
        the  left of [10X->[110X in the third line. If [10Xv1[110X corresponds to the vertex [23X(w,
        [g_{u_w}]_w)[123X  and  [10Xv2[110X  to  the vertex [23X(w', [g_{\tilde{u}_{w'}}]_{w'})[123X,
        then [10Xgam[110X corresponds to the translation [23X\gamma[123X such that[133X
  
  
  [24X      [33X[0;6Yg_{\tilde{u}_{w'}} = g_{u_w}\gamma.[133X
  
  [124X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6YThe faces are given as a list [10X<faces>[110X with entries of the form [10X[ edge,
        orientation  ][110X,  where [10Xedge[110X is the position of the edge in [10X<edges>[110X and
        [10Xorientation[110X  is  [10X1[110X or [10X-1[110X depending on whether the edge is traversed in
        the same or opposite direction as the edge given in [10X<edges>[110X.[133X

  [4X[32X  Example  [32X[104X

    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> [ <pc generator1>, <pc generator2>, ... ][128X[104X

    [4X[28X[ g1, g2, ... ] -> [ <sc generator1>, <sc generator2>, ... ][128X[104X
